About Agro Jaya



Agro Jaya is a startup in the field of coffee farming, where coffee bean products are managed in the Gunung Halu area, West Bandung Regency, West Java. The company's focus is to produce high quality coffee beans and will now expand its business sector in the technology sector.

Vision

The vision of Agro Jaya Global Indo is to become a company engaged in the agribusiness sector that is leading in the world and building a level Badan Pusat Statistik (2020).

Missions

Improving agricultural governance properly and correctly

Building human resources (HR) that are prosperous, skilled, tough and dignified.

Responsible for building and maintaining natural and social resources in the agricultural sector.

Opening the market widely by following various technological developments actively and innovatively.
